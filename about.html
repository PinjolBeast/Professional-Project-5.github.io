OUR HISTORY

From Small Iron Workshop to Global Innovator

MODERNIA was founded in 2010 in a small garage in Jakarta. Starting from concern over the high rate of tree felling for furniture, our founders began experimenting with **hollow iron** and **tempered glass**.

In 2020, MODERNIA became a pioneer in the use of carbon materials and recycled polymers. Entering 2026, MODERNIA has adopted the principle of *circular design*, where 70% of materials come from digitally monitored recycling processes.

Our Vision

To become a world leader in sustainable furniture that inspires a carbon-free lifestyle by 2030.

Our Mission

To produce high-quality furniture using durable, aesthetic, and fully recyclable non-wood materials.

Our Material Innovations

Extreme Durability

Stainless steel and aluminum are designed to last for decades without the risk of termites or weathering.

Zero Deforestation

MODERNIA does not use natural wood, preserving forest conservation for future generations.

Circular Economy

MODERNIA products can be repaired, upgraded, or recycled back into raw materials.
